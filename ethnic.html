description

Persian: The majority ethnic group, making up around 60-65% of Iran's population. They speak Persian (Farsi) and are predominantly Shia Muslim.
2. Azerbaijani: The second-largest ethnic group, primarily located in northwestern Iran, with Azerbaijani as their language.
3. Kurdish: Mainly residing in the western part of Iran, they speak Kurdish and are predominantly Sunni Muslims.
4. Arab: Found in southwestern Iran, particularly in Khuzestan province. They speak Arabic and are primarily Shia Muslims.
5. Lur: In the southwestern and western regions, they speak Luri and are predominantly Shia Muslims.
6. Baloch: In southeastern Iran, they speak Balochi and are primarily Sunni Muslims.
7. Turkmen: Located in the northeastern part of Iran, they speak Turkmen.
8. Gilaki and Mazandarani: These ethnic groups are found along the Caspian Sea coast in the north and speak Gilaki and Mazandarani languages.
9. Armenian: Primarily residing in northwest Iran, particularly in Isfahan and Tehran, they speak Armenian and are predominantly Christians.
10. Assyrian and Chaldean: Found mainly in western Iran, they speak Aramaic and are predominantly Christian.
11. Jewish: While a small minority, Iran has a historic Jewish community primarily living in cities like Tehran, Isfahan, and Shiraz
